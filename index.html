About This Site

PageWork is a showcase and playground for web technologies, JavaScript tools, and interactive demos. Whether you're looking for inspiration, useful snippets, or just want to see what's possible with modern web development, you've come to the right place!

Everything here is open source and built for learning, experimenting, and sharing ideas with the world. Feel free to fork, remix, or contribute!
